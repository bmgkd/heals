1.Privacy policy

Introduction

Ensuring your privacy and security is our top priority. In order to provide our services, we collect your username, email, and password and the reason of your depression. Our Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information we collect

We collect your username, password, and email information for the following purposes:
  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and sending important updates.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To personalize your experience and provide content and offers tailored to your interests.
We would like to clarify that we only collect information about your mental health condition or the reason for your depression if you feel comfortable sharing it with us. We respect your privacy and understand that discussing personal topics like mental health can be difficult for some individuals. Our main goal is to provide a platform where people who feel hopeless or are in pain can feel safe and supported. We believe in fostering a positive and peaceful environment for all our users, and we are constantly working towards this goal. Thank you for trusting us with your personal information, and please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or concerns.

  • Personalizing your experience on our website.
  • Analyzing user behavior to improve our content and services.
  • Enhancing the functionality and usability of our website.
